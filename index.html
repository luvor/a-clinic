What does CT-Guided Ozone cure?

The procedure can end all symptoms that are a consequence of nerve root pressure that is exerted by a herniated/protruded disc. These include leg and foot symptoms (pain, numbness, tingling, lack of sensitivity, lack of control, spasms); and can also include back symptoms (pain and back spasms). A prognosis of the result you might expect from treatment is provided by one of our surgeons following a clinical and radiological evaluation of your specific case.

About us

Ontario MediLink coordinates advanced non-OHIP eligible treatment programs that require collaborative care across its network of specialists. We provide the required linkages between physician participants, ensuring effective continuity of patient care. Our model further enables participating physicians to focus on doing what they do best ~ providing high quality health services within the scope of their expertise.
